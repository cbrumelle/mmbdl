We are the Marin Mountain Bike Defense League and we need your help! Subscribe now to get alerted about important events where you can make a difference for Mountain Biking in Marin!



You may be wondering who we are... We are teachers, doctors, neighbors, and friends. We are mothers, fathers, sisters, brothers. We are taxpayers and voters... and we want proportional and fair access to mountain bike trails in Marin.

Wherever singletrack is threatened in Marin, we will defend it. Whenever justice for Marin mountain bikers is in jeopardy, we will be there. We are the Marin Mountain Bike Defense League.

Because Mountain Biking Is Not A Crime

How It Works

Sign Up

Sign up on our mailing list to get notified when something important regarding mountain biking in Marin is about to go down.

Get Alerts

We'll send you perodic alerts about things like a possible trail closure, an upcoming town hall meeting or a hit piece in the local media that needs our attention.

Take Action

We'll make it as easy as possible for you to take action and maximize the results of your efforts.

Tell the world

Or if not the world, at least tell other mountain bikers in Marin! We'll help you throw up the bat signal whenver things are going sideways.

Questions, Suggestions, Concerns, Fan Mail?

We'd love to talk to you about Mountain Biking in Marin, trail access issues, maintenance, and things we can do to make Marin generally better for mountain bikers. Give us a shout if you'd like to chat – or better – go for a ride!
